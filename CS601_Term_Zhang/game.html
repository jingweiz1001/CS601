Games I currently played

Diablo 4

Diablo IV is the highly anticipated next installment in Blizzard Entertainment's legendary Diablo franchise. The game returns to the series' dark and gritty roots, promising a deep, immersive world steeped in gothic horror. The art style reflects this return to darkness, featuring a raw, visceral aesthetic inspired by the first two entries in the series. In Diablo IV, players will face off against the forces of hell that have overrun the world of Sanctuary. The game will introduce new classes along with the familiar ones, each with unique abilities and playstyles. As of now, the confirmed classes are Barbarian, Sorceress, Druid, and Rogue, with each class offering deep customization options. The game is set in an expansive, contiguous open world that spans diverse regions, each with unique monsters and inhabitants. From the desolate, demon-infested wastelands to the claustrophobic, narrow streets of towns, each environment is filled with danger and opportunities for the brave adventurer. Diablo IV also expands on its social features. While players can still choose to play solo, they will occasionally encounter other adventurers in the open world. There are also public events and world bosses that require teamwork to overcome. Diablo IV will feature a deep and engaging storyline, delving into the lore and legends of Sanctuary. The game promises a dark and desperate battle against the forces of hell, with a narrative that pulls no punches in its depiction of the struggle between good and evil.

Release Date: June 5 2023

Zelda:tears of the kingdom

Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom is a mesmerizing journey that takes the franchise to new heights. Its stunning visuals, intricate puzzles, and deep storytelling make it a must-play for any Zelda fan. The kingdom has never been so expansive, and each new region brings its own unique challenges and surprises. The game strikes a balance between exploration, combat, and puzzle-solving. Its world is teeming with secrets and hidden treasures, making every journey an adventure. The combat system is robust and engaging, while the puzzles are cleverly designed to challenge the player's perception and problem-solving skills. Overall, Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom is a breathtaking masterpiece that sets a new standard for adventure games. It is an experience that will stay with you long after the credits roll.

Release Date: May 12 2023
